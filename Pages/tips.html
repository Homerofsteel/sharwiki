The Simpsons Hit And Run

Tips for Speedrunning SHAR

  • Don't run while pressing a button like in 1-4, it will cause weird things.
  • Despawn traffic with caution.
  • Don't rush through your menuing.
  • Don't risk getting hit and run for one coin if you're not in a good situation.
  • Focus on your driving skills first before implementing risky strategies that only save 2 seconds.
  • Learn a proper coin route and backup coins.
  • Don't bother resetting for small time losses in early game, no-reset runs are very useful.
  • Practice and analyze what went wrong during your session to improve.
  • Don't be afraid to learn Homer 2 OOB when starting; there are very simple ones.
  • If you struggle with a category PB, try playing another one to improve your skills and knowledge.
  • Doing ILs is also a great way to improve at shar.
  • Don't always hold accelerate; learn when to brake for better speed. Some turns make your car go crazy if you only accelerate.
  • Don't play on high FPS if you can't consistently get the hard unique tricks.
  • Learn backup strategies for missions because anything can happen in this game.
  • Ensure your settings are correct; an invalid run could be the result of bad settings.
  • Don't rush when buying clothes, especially Apu-American!!!
  • Be aware of common crashes to avoid them.
  • If your hit-and-run meter is high and your coins are low, you can try to get hit-and-run in a good place to reset it.
  • Hitting solid objects like poles or trees will slow you down; try to hit them with the side of your car.
  • If your car starts flying in a wrong direction, don't be afraid to press the reset button to respawn on the road.
  • In most missions, the closer you are to NPC cars, the faster they'll go. But sometimes, slow down to avoid them from getting stuck in dumps or getting a two-cycle.
  • Find your preferred camera for driving.
