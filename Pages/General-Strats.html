The Simpsons Hit And Run

Enter Skip Storage or ESS is a technique used to skip cameras-pan-in.

Ebrake Boosting is a technique to get the fastest acceleration possible.

Cutscenes Skip

Main Menu Warp or MMW is the fact of returning to the Main Menu after a mission. This has the advantage of bringing back wasp collision and your default car. But it loses 3s compared to a normal mission warp.

Freebee is the name given to the wasps having collision that you can collect with your car.

Out Of Bounds or OOB means being out of the map, these OOBs can be very useful in a speedrun.

Wonky driving is a car state caused by a glitch which mostly badly effects your controls.

Tilted Baby Van is a state caused by a bug where the baby van car in the mission Eight Is Too Much can be titled when it spawns badly affecting his driving.

Ghost Police Car is a glicth which lets you drive an altered police car with excellent stats useful for some some categories.

Jumpzoneis a zone used to give huge boosts to a car to help clearing a jump. We can abuse it to permanently have jumpzone effect.

Restricted is the name of the main categories (except for Any%) where variable fps and timeskip are banned.

Unrestricted is the name of the categories where variable fps and timeskip are allowed.

Walking On Air is a tech which allows you to run on the air.

CPS means coin per second

Timeskip is a trick which lets you clip through walls.

ASM means All Story Missions

ARNLR means All Tasks No Level Returns

Hundo means 100%

NG+ means New Game +

NMW means No Mission Warps

IL means Individual Level

IM means Individual Mission

TAS means Tool-Assisted-Speedrun
