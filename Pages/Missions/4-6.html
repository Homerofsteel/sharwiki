The Cola Wars

One of the few on foot missions.

You can skip the little prompt saying you need to buy Police if you manage to activate the costumes fast enough.

Skip

No Skip

After starting the mission you want to restart it to save one second. If you are doing Late Inmate you'll have to get neighbor stuff.

You can perform another walking on air at the playground. This one saves less than a second.

Late Inmate: Approach the Kwik-E-Mart wasp, it will start to follow you. Then get to the next wasp and kill everything.

Early Inmate: If you're lucky enough you can perform a double kill with the box and the wasp.

Then just finish your collection of cola cans and enter Kwik-E-Mart at the end of the mission.
