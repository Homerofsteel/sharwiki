Redneck Roundup

A long follow mission.

Get traileryard wasp as a freebee.

You can get both powerplant vends. If you're not confident you can also get the first vend in 4-7.

You can also grind gold house box for 20 more coins but don't go for it if you're too far away.

Nothing much to say for the rest of the mission. Just get maximum of coins during the mission without failing.
If you're doing Late Inmate route, you need to get in your car at the end of the mission and go buying Inmate at Kwik-E-Mart. Then Main Menu Warp.

Try to get one or two wasps of neighbors and go get gas station wasp. This can be really tight so learn how to deal with the shortcut with the bush turn.

There are two main strats for donut truck.
The first one is simply blocking Donut Truck path and wait for him to ram into you. If he get out, cath up and do the same thing again.
The second one consists of pushing the Donut Truck near the house and to ram him. Exit your car to collect the drops and reset your car.
