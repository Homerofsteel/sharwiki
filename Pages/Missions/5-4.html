This Little Piggy

A follow Wiggum mission with a cool tricky strat

You can get hospital stuff during the mission. The wasp is a little crazy so the best way to get it is to pause the game when getting the first box and make sure the wasp is on the lowest point of the cycle and try turning in the direction the wasp is supposed to be for more chances.

After collecting the last donut you can get police station wasp as a freebee and instantly reset your car to be able to get ESS. If you go for next trick timing can be tricky so you might want to skip ESS.

The following strat is a way to setup jumpzone to make the next strat a lot easier and faster. You simply need to park your car on the ramp and re enter your car when it's at the bottom of the ramp. Then go to the left of the Sit-N-Rotate to spawn the wasp to make sure monorail wasp will cooperate.

The jumpzone you setuped before makes the following push a lot easier. Before that, we want to despawn the traffic to makes it easier. The first one is done in first person just after monorail and the second one is done in third person just after the turn. When pushing him the best way to conserve your speed is to be parallel to the shortcut wall. Because you need to push him a second time to make sure he pass by the shortcut. Otherwise there are chances he goes on the initial road losing a lot of time.

At the end of the mission get the vend and the wrench which will makes you lose jumpzone.

Notes:You may have the glitched police car setuped in the previous mission and the route is the same.
