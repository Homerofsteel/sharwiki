Operation Hellfish

This mission is a consistent destruction mission

Here's a cool manip to get a traffic car faster. It's pretty bad if you mess it up so don't worry too much about it. Pretty much zoom on the camera and hold left on the camera, hold up right for like 3 sec when you're in the foliage.

When you're in the traffic car, depending of your coins you can either get Kamp Krusty or skip it. It is not recommended to get Kamp Krusty with Ford Focus or Fish Van because they don't easily go over the rocks. For the warp you need to park your car near the river, exit your car and run in the river before the iris wipe ends. This should warp you next to Otto.

Warp

Kamp Krusty

For the first car, get the red hat and reverse, wait until the sedan passes you. Then gently push it to the tree, that should do it. Otherwise push it on the bridge.

When approaching the end of the road, hold back your camera which will normally spawn a car on your left. This will manip the car so it will always do the right option. If you're lucky enough the sedan can enter the shortcut and you may be able to kill it before the log blocks you.
