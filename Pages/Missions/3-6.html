Fishy Deals

Pretty linear mission where you need to collect fishes on the way.

In this mission you're able to collect lighthouse wasp as a freebee however it is very difficult to get consistently, you want to make sure that the wasp is at his lowest point when the front of your car is at his range. Then after getting lighthouse fish, point your car to the boat and reset your car to spawn near the next fish.

After the fourth fish go get the box on your right.

The rest of the mission is pretty linear and not too hard just make sure to avoid traffic.

Skipping The Mission

Fishy Deals is one of the missions where you get the most coins in any%. Here's one of the fastest coin route. This route also lets you get boat coins if you're doing fast limo in 3-7. Don't try to get last box if you know that it will take additional time after skipping the mission.

Not Skipping The Mission

This is not recommended unless you're going for a very good time!!! Just here for reference.
It is possible with extremely good coin route along the run to not skip Fishy Deals which saves about 45 sec but requires a very precise and kinda inconsistent timeskip hallfway through. The beginning of the mission is the same as other categories.
In this mission you're able to collect lighthouse wasp as a freebee however it is very difficult to get consistently, you want to make sure that the wasp is at his lowest point when the front of your car is at his range. Then after getting lighthouse fish, point your car to the boat and reset your car to spawn near the next fish.

After the fourth fish go get the box on your right.

Here's the big part about not skipping the mission, krustylu is worth to get in any case and so this timeskip is mandatory to be able to get a fast enough coin route to make this route worth. This timeskip is really weird because it's hard to have control of your car when you're in that oob spot and so we can only pray.

There's a second timeskip much easier than the first one saving a few seconds.

Nothing much to say about this mission in NG+, just make sure to not go too fast in some turns to not get launched and get School Bus at the end of the mission.

Full Mission Example

After the fourth fish go get the box on your right.

After the tenth fish reverse and push the brewery mascot to easily get the first wasp. If you're using Wasp Cycle you can try getting the second wasp as a freebee too. Otherwise exit your car and kill it, do the same for the third wasp, reset your car and continue the mission.

Near the end of the mission, stick to the wall to spawn the wasp, that will make the next wasp a lot easier to kill.

At the end of the mission you'll have to get dam stuff, start with getting the card. if you're not confident with doing walking in air like shown below you can re-enter your car and go to the entrance. Get everything and warp to 3-7.
