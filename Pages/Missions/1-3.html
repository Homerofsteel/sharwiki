Office Spaced

This is the first destruction mission. The mission consists of destroying Smithers.

Lenny Push

Pushing Lenny permits to skip the getting in the car → drive → getting out of the car saving a couple of seconds.
Notes: Look back before pushing Lenny to make sure that Lenny doesn't dive away
Talking to Barney is faster because the car requirement prompt doesn't appear
Restarting mission here is faster than running to the car

Plow King 1-3 is the fastest way of destroying Smithers.
1. Simply push Smithers in the corner.
2. This strat is very hard and not the most consistent. You need to reset at the exact same moment as in the video and Smithers should slowly starts to accelerate then you need to find the perfect angle to block him on the wall and then hit him for the right amount of times to kill him. That also lets you get ESS.

1. Beginner-Intermediate

2. Advanced

Traffic Car is a safer alternative and also is a lot better than Plow King on 60fps.
1. Simply push Smithers in the corner.
2. Groundpounding the car before entering it to be able to explode it on Smithers.

1. Beginner-Intermediate

2. Advanced

Smithers Strat

Fastest way to destroy Smithers in NG+.
Notes: If you miss this strat you can do the same one as Plow King by pushing Smithers in the corner of the house.

Lenny Push

Pushing Lenny permits to skip the getting in the car → drive → getting out of the car saving a couple of seconds.
Notes: Looking back before pushing Lenny to make sure that Lenny doesn't dive away.
You can get ess while failing the mission.

You can keep Plow King in Any%, after you restarted 6 times the mission you can reverse and get Krusty Burgers coins then go get gas stations coins. Then fail the mission by entering any car from the phonebooth.

Notes: There's a chance that you enter the car normally and don't fail the mission. In that case simply enter in another car from the phonebooth or in a traffic car.
