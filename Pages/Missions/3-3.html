Bonfire Of The Manatees

Pretty hard for beginners, very easy for better players

Starting the mission, hold back your camera until 1:58 appears on the timer. Then push Cletus to the Arcade then brake to make sure he's stuck. Then just do some ebrake boost to collect the drops as fast as possible. When you get all 15 drops reset your car.

If you hold right for a few just before the slope starts you can a huge boost but don't worry too much about it if you miss it.

When approaching the Observatory go in first person and when the screen go black hold up, run and mash the enter button to enter the Observatory. After talking to Frink go buy Cool Lisa at the back of the Observatory.

After talking to Milhouse you can get ess.

You can get Hit N Run when you're on the pier to make the police car explode next to the box on the beach. If you're lucky enough you might be able to get the second box if you wait a few seconds near Planet Hype.

In NMW, don't park your car near Milhouse otherwise your car will despawn. That's why you need to push Milhouse before exiting your car.

You can do two differents strats here.
1st: If you have cars spawned in front of you just take one of them, fail the mission and get in front of Android Dungeon door, warp to 3-2 and get School Bus from the phonebooth.
2nd: If no cars in front of you spawn get one at your left, reverse and get School Bus from the phonebooth. The second strat is slower because you don't get fast iris wipes during 3-4.
