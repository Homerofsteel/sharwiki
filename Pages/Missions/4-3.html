Ketchup Logic

A very satisfying mission to do fast.

The first jump on the fire truck can be pretty tricky.

If you're doing Late Inmate you can get chessboard wasp as a freebee.

Here's one of the hardest strats to get consistently. This trick use a speed boost to clear the jump without momentum.

If you go for this trick miss it with no speed you can just turn and get some speed to be able to clear the jump.

If you have good speed you can go through the field and then through the barn. That also spawns barn wasp which will makes back of school wasp have collision.

If you're doing early inmate just warp to the next mission, otherwise run to the phonebooth and get Malibu Stacy.
