Alien 'Auto'Topsy II

Drive oob and reset your car when you see the dot on the minimap on your left.

Low Fps Only

The following strat can only be performed on fps preferably under 80 because of how collisions work. After resetting your car drive until the map loads and turn around and drive until the bridge.

When you get to the bridge park your car on the right fence to make it so it is perpendicular to it. Charge an ebrake boost and you should be able to clip the bridge.

The optimal strat here when you're oob is to reset your car when you're at the same level as the end of the road. Then hold accelerate, brake and ebrake and hold left until your car is somewhat tilting so that means you'll get infinite speed. Otherwise reset later so you'll have more time preparring it by reversing. Or if you don't want to do any infinitespeed start just reverse until you're on the road. When you return to the school don't reset your car otherwise you'll not be able to talk to grandpa.

Jumpzone

After resetting your car drive until the map loads and turn around and drive until the bridge. If you want to get the fastest alien car, you want to drive for a few on the grass around the grey path. Then you'll need to park precisely on the jump in the playground area. Ideally you want to park you car just in front of the pumpkin and to the right of the other pumpkin. Exit your car and jump onto it, normally the alien car should push your car and you'll get jumpzone. Now go get to the graveyard shortcut.

When you're at the graveyard shortcut align your car with the pole on your right. Then charge an ebrake boost and release it when you're at the level of the second pole. You'll need to press left two times pretty quickly.

The optimal strat here when you're oob is to reset your car when you're at the same level as the end of the road.
