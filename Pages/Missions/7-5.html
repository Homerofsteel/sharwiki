Alien 'Auto'Topsy I

Easy

After talking to Burns and getting the map, fall in a trap and go get a traffic car preferably the skeleton one. Get the waste and if you correctly parked your car you should be able to enter it.

Follow the dot on the minimap to get back to the school. You now need to find the traffic car you used to get the waste, enter it and go to the beam.

OOB Tutorial

Hard

If your car got damaged too much you'll need to restart the mission. After talking to Burns and getting the map, fall in a trap end enter your car. Now for this oob there are a lot of differents way to it. One easy one consists of lining up your car with the white lines on the floor. Then reverse until you touch the waste, press accelerate just before touching the wall and hold right after hitting the wall. You should end up on the wall now you'll need to prepare an ebrake boost whil having your front wheels on the upper part of the pipe while making sure the cage will not stop your car.

Follow the dot on the minimap and enter the beam while avoiding snake.
