Never Trust A Snake

The longest mission of the game

The first part of the mission consists of following a garbage truck but this car has some problems to drive so we have to help him to save time. A The end of the road the garbage truck will try to turn left losing precious time so we are going to push him on the other side like this:

You still want to continue to manip him by gently pushing him to make his driving as straight as possible.

After collecting the last drop from the garbage truck head to the monorail to collect the next drops on foot. Kick the wasp on your way to kill it when returning to your car. On the monorail take as many coins as you need but prefer avoiding them because they are very slow to get.

When returning to your car from the monorail you can perform some walking on air saving some time.

Then we're heading towards the 4 drops roof. To clear the jump you want to stop accelerate when on the slope so you'll park your car next to the vents. Here get the drops and kill the wasp if you need it.

After that reverse and get the drops in the tunnel and near the firetruck.

To collect the lasts drops you want to first get the ones on the ramp and reset while being on the left side to respawn near the next drops.

This route involves a difficult but consistent jump later and is only recommended for advanced players and saves like 5 seconds. If you're doing this route don't get ESS in 5-4.
After collecting the last drop from the garbage truck get the Sit-N-Rotate drops and then the 4 drops on the roof.

After that reverse and get the drops in the tunnel and near the firetruck.

It's now time for the ramp jump. You want to park your car on the left side of the ramp and lineup Apu with the end of the fence. Then perform an ebrake boost, when you see the car starts to tilt upwards then release ebrake, after collecting the second drop on the ramp hold right and you should land on the box. If you miss the jump just go get monorail normally.

When you landed on monorail get the drops and go get your car back from the phonebooth.

The fastest way to go back to Snake is to pass by the highway shortcut and reset when you're at the turn after the jump like this example:

When approaching the DMV you can push Snake closer to the entrance.

After ending the mission warp to 4-1 and warp back to 5-6.
