The Old Pirate And The Sea

A pretty hard mission for beginners and intermediate players.

School Bus

This strat is recommended for newcomers and for people playing under 100 fps.
You'll have to get School Bus after Fishy Deals. What you want do after entering the car is to place yourself in the spot at your left and prepare an ebrake boost waiting the limo. Then push him from behind to the wall.

Backup

If you don't manage to get the Limo stuck on the wall that's not a big deal, just push the Limo into the entrance of the beach area. You can get beach boxes at the same time if you wish and reset your car near the Old Pirate.

To get to the boat with School Bus you'll need one of the poles. The one just befores the Springfield sign is harder than the next one but gives you a wasp.

Phonebooth Limo

These strats are only doable on 100+fps!!!
This is the fastest way to kill the Limo in 3-7. You can either use Mr. Plow or Malibu Stacy for this strats. Mr.Plow tends to be easier but slower. After entering your car go on the right side of the pier to makes the Limo goes right. Then you'll be able to get on his way and push him near the phonebooth. You want to lineup the left side of the Limo with the black line you can see on the building. Wait for a few that the Limo reverse and goes forward. When he's touching the wall get Mr.Plow from the phonebooth, now you can finish the Limo with a groundpound. Get back Malibu Stacy for the next trick.

Boat Jump

Doing phonebooth Limo let's you perform another hard trick called Boat Jump. This one is pretty though and everyone has his own lineup for it. You can approach it from the left and turn right just before hitting the pole to get launched in the air.

When you're on the boat get the two boxes and try getting the wasp as a freebee if you know that it's a freebee. Click here to get all possible combinations to make this wasp a freebee: Wasp routing
