Curious Curator

A tough mission with the scariest cutscene skip

You can get courthouse box and gazebo before heading to the curator.

The main strat to destroy curator is to hit him on the front of his car and makes him get stuck on the stature. Then you have to perform an ebrake boost to do a lot of damage at once. Doing small hits can make him escape.

Backup

If you miss the previous strat you can go for this easy backup. When the curator is about to turn push him and it should end up on the wall.

Tutorial

After destroying the curator there's another cutscene skip. This one is very scary because it skip a 2m20 cutscene. One particularity of this cutscene skip is that you can't pause buffer at first but if you alt tab or press the windows key and go back to the game you'll be able to do it.
