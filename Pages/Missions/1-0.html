Cola Caper

Cola Caper is the tutorial mission of The Simpsons Hit And Run. It introduces us into Level 1 while playing as Homer. The goal is simple: collect ice at the Kwik-E-Mart.

First thing you want to do after talking to Marge is jumping on your car to enter it. This is the fastest way to enter a car as you can see with the iris wipe which skips any animation of opening one of the car doors.

There are two options to go to the Kwik-E-Mart in a run:

Blue House's Shorcut

This is the most common way to go to the Kwik-E-Mart. Simply takes the blue house shorcut that you can see at the first turn at your left.
Notes: There is a optimal way to get this shortcut depending on the boosts you get. The key is slowing down at the right time and slowly turn left to get the first boost which helps to cross the second jump easily. Here's a little comparison between no boost and boost:

Boost

Bush Strat

Description: This is the most optimal route for Cola Caper. It's recommended to learn this pretty early especially if you're on high fps which makes it easier. Bush strat allows you to get neighbors coins straigthforward.
How to perform it:After you have entered the Family sedan quickly turn left behind the blue house, then turn right and try to aim at the bush while going gently on the left. Normally you should be able to climb the bush. At the peak of your climb reset your car and reverse to the Kwik-E-Mart. Try to aim at the vending machine.
