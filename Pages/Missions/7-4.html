There's Something About Monty

As before, this mission will vary on if you choose the easy way or the faster but harder way to get OOB.

Easy

After finishing 7-3 run to the zombie who hosts the race. Kick when Frink says "it" after he says "ohhhh" to skip the mission complete animation. Start the race cancel it and then enter the school.

After talking to Lisa get back to your car and drive oob in direction of the powerplant. When you get close to it, approach to the back of the powerplant and park your car so left front or right front of your car touches the wall a little to the right of the fence. Then when you're correctly parked jump in direction of the powerplant and you should spawn in bounds. If your character is stuck in the wall give a few kicks in multiple directions.

After that climb all the way to Burns office. At the end of the climb when you're on the last platform jump and alt tab/Windows to not get stuck in place. Then run to Burns while avoiding falling in the traps. The correct path is: Right, Left, Left, Right, Left.

OOB Tutorial

Hard

Warp to 7-4, talk to Lisa and restart the mission. Get Zombie Car and go to the fence on the baseball field.

Park your car on the left of the fence, start an ebrake boost and lineup with the right side of the area code sign. Release brake first then release ebrake when your car is starting to tilt upwards. Then hold left when the camera changes and finally stop holding left when you're about to jump. Release accelerate in the air and hold it again before landing.

After that, climb all the way to Burns office. At the end of the climb when you're on the last platform jump and alt tab/Windows to not get stuck in place. Then run to Burns while avoiding falling in the traps. The correct path is: Right, Left, Left, Right, Left.

OOB Tutorial

This page covers how to get oob in 7-2, it is only viable for +150 fps so if you're playing on lower fps just follow the car for the whole mission making sure to slow down after passing the bridge. Also if you're not doing oob in this mission you can get barn stuff at the end while waiting alien car to complete the mission.

You first want to get the school playground card

If you're not too far away you can get the krusty burger box.

For the first half of the mission you want to follow the alien car pretty close to make it go faster. When're you're about to enter the Simpsons house area take the shortcut and go to the graveyard shortcut to setup an oob.

When you're at this point, break the tree, reverse and setup an ebrake boost. Release brake until the pillar on your right, you want to tap left two times after you pass the first tree. It's hard to tell precisely how to do it so your best bet is to try and find your favorite way to do it.

Once you're oob you'll need to carefully drive to specifics points to avoid failing the mission, first align your car with the road on your left on the minimap, exit your car and when the bar starts going up enter your car and start driving. AFter that make sure to not be driving at the right of the part of the road you can see on the floor. Continue driving aiming for the middle of the broken model while making sure you're not too far forwards and reset when you're about Frink's level.
