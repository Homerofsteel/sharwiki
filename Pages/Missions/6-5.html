Full Metal Jackass

After warping to this mission you want to get ess. If you had ess warping to this mission you'll need slow down a bit to make sure the car from the phonebooth will not spawn on Skinner.

Hit him once and turn around, prepare an ebrake boost and release it when skinner is turning.

Easy Strat

Just before Skinner turns near krustylu push him and when you're in the area slow down to be able to get the big hit.

Fast Strat

This strat is more risky than the other one and only saves about 2-3 seconds. What you want to do is start pushing skinner when he's getting on the last hill but you also need to manage your speed. Going too fast when pushing him will be harder to stick to the car and to be able to get a proper push.
