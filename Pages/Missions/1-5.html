Flowers By Irene

Flowers By Irene is the first follow mission of the game. One important mechanic to understand is rubberbanding. Simply the closer you are from the black van the faster he'll be but sometimes you'll intentionnally slow down to make him not stuck at some places.

Restarting the mission is also faster than exiting the house+ pausing unpausing makes the van not reversing saving even more time.

Getting Krusty Burger in 1-5 is the most optimal way to get all the coins. Then make sure to stay on the left of the bridge to not spawn the wasp.

If you spawned the wasps correctly you should be able to get this wasp as a freebee.

Make sure to look back before the van passes the barn shortcut.

You can also look back before the power plant to get the fastest follow possible.

You should be able to get stonecutters wasp as a freebee too. Getting it as a freebee will let you get ESS, just wait on foot near the phonebooth to end the mission. If your coin route is not optimized you can stop your car at the stonecutters to get one more wasp.
You can enter your car during the black screen to get 3 coins instead of 2 :)
