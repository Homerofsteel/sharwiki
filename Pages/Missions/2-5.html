Better Than Beef

First mission with the great Cletus.

There isn't much to say about the beginning it's pretty easy just learn your own looking back to deal with a better traffic. The fastest route consists of taking this shortcut

Skipping this item let's you take a sharper turn.

Then you have to get the item behind the train, just choose between the two next followings option. Also you can clip through the train to save more time but this can be pretty risky.

Next we have the most difficult part of the mission with Apu Escape. Turn around the Krusty Burger and look at where is Apu on the map to check if that's a slow or a fast escape. You can also get gazebo during the escape, it's not the fastest coins stack to pick but is a good choice for beginners.
