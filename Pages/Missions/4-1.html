First mission with Marge

Level 4 has two main routes, and this change a lot of little things throughout the level. Make sure to remember which route you're are doing. More information here: Marge routing

The two main routes differences start on the fisrt mission with the number of wasps you get.
Early Inmate: Get only one wasp of neighbors and get Krusty Burger vend.
Late Inmate: Get the two wasps of neighbors by holding right when touching the wall with your car inclined to the left.
