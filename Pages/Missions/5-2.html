And Baby Makes Eight

The mission where you have to escape the mafia.

The first Limo is very easy to escape so just go to the fountain near the hospital. Then reset when hitting the pillar to make sure you trigger the second Limo. To get the next wasp as a freebee we are going to wait until the wasp cycle is almost at the top right before starting accelerating. The stairs have weird collision but the best way to avoid hitting them is to angle your car at 90°.

After getting the wasp and the box head to Homer to buy Car Built For Homer and that will complete the mission on the way.

After buying Car Built For Homer warp to 4-1 and warp back to 5-3 to be able to do a easy fast strat for the next mission.
