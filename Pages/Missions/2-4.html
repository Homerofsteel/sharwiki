Bart'N'Frink

Hardest Level 2 mission close with Cellouts.

When starting the mission, quickly enter the sports car to start an ebrake boost to be ahead of the black van to try the push.Push him until you hit the tree and then wait until he passes the intersection. This will make him go right which makes the next strat a lot easier.

If you sucessfully manip him he should be going on the right side so you can push him at the right time to pass through the dirt jump with the black van.

You can then get car wash wasp and trainyard wasps for a pretty good cps.

Near the end of the follow section you can get Herman box, you'll respawn near Wiggums which makes it a good deal.

There are two possibilities to complete this race:
Highway jump: This strat works better on high fps and can be risky.
Reset: A simpler strat, you just need to reset just before the turn but loses like 3-4 secondes.
