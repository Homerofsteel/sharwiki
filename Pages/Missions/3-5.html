Slithery Sleuthing

Hardest mission of Lisa and RNG dependant

Wait until Snake drives on the crossroads to start driving, that makes so Snake doesn't get stuck later. Get the vend on the way.

At this turn, try pushing the back of Snake car to readjust him so he doesn't get stuck on the stairs.

After the stairs get ahead of him and block him before the road starts to descend this will make prevent the last drop to spawn after the bridge losing huge amount of time.

If you're fast enough you'll be able to fit in the log space but don't play it too risky if you're not confident.

When the mission is done run to the phonebooth to get ESS but don't get here too fast because the phonebooth will not be usable.
