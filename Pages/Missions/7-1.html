Rigor Motors

The first mission of Homer 2 and the only one without any oob.
You'll have to think of how many coins you need to afford the Zombie car:
+495 coins: a few props on the way, 430-495 coins: only krusty burger, 410-430 coins: neighbors, -410: neighbors and krusty burger
You also might just want to get the krusty burger box depending on your coins.

If you need neighbors coins get them at the very start of the mission.

After talking to Lisa exit the house, enter your car and go talk to Flanders. After skipping the dialogue hold down left so you can get the medkit during the dialogue transition.

If you need more coins you can get the krusty burger coins, you might be able to skip the vend if you know how many coins you need.

Go buy Zombie Car after completing the mission.

Full Mission Example

Get TV gag, exit the house , then the first thing you'll get is neighbors coins with the wasp as a freebee then go get Wiggums backyard wasp.

After restarting the mission, get Flanders stuff, simpsons stuff and buy the outfits if you already did coin grinding.

After talking to Lisa exit the house, enter your car and go talk to Flanders. After skipping the dialogue hold down left so you can get the medkit during the dialogue transition.

If you're confident in having enough time you can get the cemetary card and instantly reset. You then want to get the bridge wasp if you have enough time. Timing can be very tight though.

After getting the Cletus item you want to get the traileryard wasps, you still want to be very quick here because the timer is still tight.

You want to get krusty burger coins on your way back.

After completing the mission go get playground stuff and then do the race.
