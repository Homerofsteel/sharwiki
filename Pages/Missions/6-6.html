Set To Kill

This mission considered as one of the hardest missions to complete casually is not that hard when you're speedrunning because it doesn't have a very strict timer.

Normal Route

Get all the stands on your way until you reach the Captain stand where you're going to take the shortcut and skip 3 drops that we're going to get later.

Before you enter the Casino area you want to flick back your camera so you don't have any cars on your way for the upcoming stand.

When you're on the ramp jump to the right and reset your car to land on the 3 drops we skipped earlier.

You'll continue your way to the boat where you'll perform another Boat Jump, this one being much easier than the one in Lisa but it's still recommended to go for it only if you're playing on +100fps.

When reaching Krusty park your car a little away from him so it doesn't despawn and get back in your car after talking to Krusty, reset you car (make sure it orientates to the left) and follow the invisible wall on your right until you can turn. This is the point where you can start driving oob to the desired location. When the dot on the minimap switches to the correct location wait a second and reset your car.
