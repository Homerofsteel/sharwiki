Bonestorm Storm

Bonestorm Storm is the first hit and collect mission of the game.

What you want to do in this mission is to get all 10 drops before the big shortcut to be optimal. Try to reproduce movement shown in the video which has some delay for errors.

You can reset your car after the shortcut pretty early after the jump.

At the end of the mission instead of getting ESS you can go in front of the Simpsons' house. This will lock your camera and will let you skip the next mission camera pan-in by pausing unpausing like the following clip is showing.
