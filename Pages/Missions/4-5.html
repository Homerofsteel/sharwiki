Wolves Stole My Pills

A long follow mission followed by another unskippable cutscene.

After talking to Nelson you're able to get back of school wasp and then reset your car.

Now we're entering follow section. One major thing to note is that you have to make sure your follow bar don't get under 1/3 because there's a chance that you can fail the mission. So try to stay close from the sedan even if that requires to skip some coins.
Near the church you want to look back, this will make the sedan turn left down here and will avoid the sedan getting stuck at the Krusty Burger.
If you're doing Early Inmate and have Canyonero you need to switch to Malibu Stacy during the follow section. The safest phonebooth is the one at the church. Switching cars will also empty your hit and run meter.

When you're entering the rich area, you want to start slowing down a bit because of a major timeloss which can happen called 2 cycles. If you stay too close from the sedan there's a chance that one the last drops will be skipped and you will not be able to get it. This means you'll have to wait that the sedan finishes his loop before getting the last item.

Normal Example

2 cycles example

After you've collected all the drops you just have to return to Grandpa. If you're having trouble escaping the sedan by taking the normal path you can go through the shortcut and reset for an easy escape.
