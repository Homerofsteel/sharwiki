Pocket Protector

There are two possible ways (3 if you're doing inbounds) of doing this mission depending on if you performed oob in 7-2.

No 7-2 OOB

After starting the mission you want to turn your car around and lineup with the left wall. Then accelerate and brake/reverse when you hit the wall and turn left. You should now be able to climb the wall.

If your car on the wall is too much on the right and might get stuck by the cage, what you should do is wiggle a lot to the left so your car is turning so you'll be able to get oob on the other side of the wall. If you see your car being stuck you want to accelerate for a bit so you don't get the forced reset.

Once you're oob follow the dot on the minimap. Here if you alternate left and right you'll get a small speed boost.
Then you have two choices: If you do the hard zombie car oobs following this mission, reset your car just after seeing the beam. Else if you do the easier following oobs don't reset your car.

With 7-2 OOB

If you don't want to go for the infinite speed strat you just have to drive oob and reset your car when you see the tractor beam.
Firstly place your top right wheel on the floor so your car gets elevated. Then reverse and put your back left wheel on the floor. Now your car should have infinite speed and now go to the school. If you want to play safe reset your car when you see the tractor beam.

This page covers how to get oob in 7-2, it is only viable for +150 fps so if you're playing on lower fps just follow the car for the whole mission making sure to slow down after passing the bridge. Also if you're not doing oob in this mission you can get barn stuff at the end while waiting alien car to complete the mission.

You first want to get the school playground card

If you're not too far away you can get the krusty burger box.

For the first half of the mission you want to follow the alien car pretty close to make it go faster. When're you're about to enter the Simpsons house area take the shortcut and go to the graveyard shortcut to setup an oob.

When you're at this point, break the tree, reverse and setup an ebrake boost. Release brake until the pillar on your right, you want to tap left two times after you pass the first tree. It's hard to tell precisely how to do it so your best bet is to try and find your favorite way to do it.

Once you're oob you'll need to carefully drive to specifics points to avoid failing the mission, first align your car with the road on your left on the minimap, exit your car and when the bar starts going up enter your car and start driving. AFter that make sure to not be driving at the right of the part of the road you can see on the floor. Continue driving aiming for the middle of the broken model while making sure you're not too far forwards and reset when you're about Frink's level.

After resetting your car destroy the Zombie Car with a groundpound and talk to Frink.
