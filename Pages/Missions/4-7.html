From Outer Space

One of the hardest destruction missions.

First thing you do is restart the mission.

This is the easiest way to kill the first Cola truck. After hitting him one front head push him near the retirement castle to block him behind the sign and finish him.

This is the most common strat used for the first Cola truck. After hitting him front head you want to try to gently push him onto the pole.

Backup

If you miss one of the previous strats you can go for the main backup known as the bush strat. Before the Cola truck starts turning push him onto the corner of the road.

If you miss this backup just try to push him onto other walls.

After some driving you'll get to the roof box by taking the gas station shortcut. This one can be pretty awkward especially on high fps. First you want to slow down a lot and place your car on the middle of the shortcut and lineup your car to the roof by turning right. This should prevent getting some weird physics on the roof.

Then we arrive to the second Cola truck, make sure to despawn traffic before triggering him. What you want to do is start pushing him when he's about to turn right at the entrance of Burn's mansion onto the corner.

Then you can get the box at the right of the chessboard entrance, if you're doing Early Inmate route you can get right chessboard wasp as a freebee.

It's time for the last truck which is probably the easiest one. When he's about to turn push him until the wall, he should stay stuck on the tree so just finish him.

On your way to the end of the mission you can get krusty burger coins.

At the end of the mission you have to escape Wiggums, simply enter the Simpsons' house and it will finish the mission. The reason is that the Simpsons' house is located very far away out of bounds.

Full Mission Example

In 100% 4-7 is the "go get everything" mission, it is the best place to get something you could have missed earlier.
When entering the Kwik-E-Mart make sure to get Larry The Looter Gag before talking to Apu because you can't enable this gag during a mission and get the remaining gags too.

This is the easiest way to kill the first Cola truck. After hitting him one front head push him near the retirement castle to block him behind the sign and finish him.

This is the most common strat used for the first Cola truck. After hitting him front head you want to try to gently push him onto the pole.

Backup

If you miss one of the previous strats you can go for the main backup known as the bush strat. Before the Cola truck starts turning push him onto the corner of the road.

If you miss this backup just try to push him onto other walls.

On your way get tower stuff.

We have next roof card, this one is pretty awkward to get. Basically stop your car on the right side of the shortcut and drive straight. If your lineup is right you should get a jump boost from the roof and collect the card. It's not too bad if you miss it.

Then we arrive to the second Cola truck, make sure to despawn traffic before triggering him. What you want to do is start pushing him when he's about to turn right at the entrance of Burn's mansion onto the corner.

If you have succesfully destroyed the second cola truck you can safely get the mansion stuff.

Then you can get the box at the right of the chessboard entrance and chessboard wasp on foot.

After that get powerplant gag and wasp. If you did not Main Menu Warp after 4-5 you can kill the wasp by jumping on top of your car. Otherwise you'll have to jump on the plateforms and kill it from the top. You can also get the vends.

It's time for the last truck which is probably the easiest one. When he's about to turn push him until the wall, he should stay stuck on the tree so just finish him.

On your way to the end of the mission get everything so Barn wasp, traileryard card and wasps, krusty burger coins and Wiggums backyard.
If you're lucky you might be able to instantly escape Wiggums like the following clip did, otherwise just run in the Simpsons' house.
