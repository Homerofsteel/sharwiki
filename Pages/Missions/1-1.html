S-M-R-T

S-M-R-T is the first real story mission of the game. You need to beat Skinner at a race to the school

Like in Cola Caper you can take two paths which are the Blue House's Shortcut and Bush Strat. Do them exactly as you would do during Cola-Caper but this time head to the Fire-Truck's vend and immediately reset your car to respawn on the next road.

Blue House's Shortcut

Bush Strat

Make sure to despawn the traffic car just after the reset.

Church and Krusty Burger boxes are really fast to get so they are usually unavoidable in a run and missing them are usually a reset.
