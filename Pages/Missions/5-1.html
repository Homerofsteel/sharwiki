Incrimating Caffeine

A very long and tight follow mission

With the current route used takes the Sit-N-Rotate wasp as a freebee at the really start of the level.

If your coins are low you can get the box near the construction area.

Go get American then.

During the mission you can get a lot of coins like stadium vends and courthouse box. You can also get them before the mission if you're not confident.

You can also get krusty burger vend.

This mission also has a chance to make a 2 cycles but it's a lot rarer. When the mission is done you can get stairs box.

If you finish Never Trust A Snake with the Limo you'll still have it when warping to 5-1 allowing you to perform Limo OOB.

Because Limo is a very slow vehicle you'll want to switch to a better car to take advantage of the rubber banding mechanic and also to get OWRC for the next mission. The best spot for that is the first phonebooth you'll see but it can be tight.
