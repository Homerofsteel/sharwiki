The Simpsons Hit And Run

Cutscenes Skip

Description: Cutscenes skip is the trick which saves the most time in the run if we don't account general gameplay. The timesave depends on the category but we can count it in minutes. There are seven differents cutscenes in the game but only four of them are currently skippable in a legal full game run.

All cutscenes: Intro cutscene: 40sec [NOT SKIPPABLE]
1-5 cutscene: 45sec [NOT SKIPPABLE]
Level 2 ending cutscene: 55sec [SKIPPABLE]
4-5 cutscene: 1m02sec [NOT SKIPPABLE]
Level 5 ending cutscene: 2m20sec [SKIPPABLE]
Level 6 ending cutscene: 43sec [SKIPPABLE]
Level 7 ending cutscene: 2min 40s

How to perform it: After completing a mission there will be a small frame window where you'll be able to pause. This frame window depends on the framerate you're playing with. The higher the fps are the harder it gets. To get a pause on the right frame start pausing nearly when the mission complete text disappear and start pausing unpausing at a good rythme to advance the game for a few frames. until you're on the right frames. Every one of these cutscenes feels different and no general cue exists for them so it's recommended to practice each of these a lot.
Special case:The Apu cutscene skip is the only one which doesn't let you pause buffering, this means you either have to pause on the right frame by mashing or you can alt tab and that will let you perform pause buffering.
